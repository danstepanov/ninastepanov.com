About Me

I am a Bostonian living in NYC, currently an Associate at Techstars IoT. I’m a generalist with a background in marketing, growth, process optimization, and partnership development. I love black tea and lifting heavy things. I’m deeply passionate about connecting people and growing startups.

I graduated with a degree in Entrepreneurship and Marketing from Northeastern University in December of 2015. While in college, I participated in the co-op program by working 3, full-time 6-month internships at Embedly (YC, acq by Medium), Intuit Quickbase, and HubSpot, all before graduating. I was on the board of the Entrepreneurs Club and IDEA: Northeastern University’s Venture Accelerator. I started my own company selling single-package protein and supplements and advised countless student startups, some of which I still work closely with today.

Since graduating, I’ve helped build Inbound.org (as part of HubSpot Labs), marketed local government software as Director of Marketing for ViewPoint Cloud, and co-founded a community for students interested in health and fitness called Fit University. In my free time, I satisfy my interest in venture capital as a contributor on the topic for VentureFizz.
